What can I ask Jeanie?

Jeanie is configured to assist with a wide range of topics. She can answer questions related to the latest government schemes, CDC Vouchers and Passion Card. She can converse in English, Chinese, Malay or Tamil — all through natural voice interaction.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed. Click the button and you're connected instantly. Conversations are session-based and not stored after you close the page.

Which browsers are supported?

Jeanie works on all modern browsers — Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. Make sure microphone permissions are enabled when prompted.

How is this powered?

Voice synthesis and conversational AI are provided by ElevenLabs' Conversational AI platform. The agent is fully customisable with different voices, personalities, and knowledge bases.
